## ARTICLE I — UNIVERSAL ACCESS

1.1 Every Super App MUST support a minimum of **100 languages** at launch.
1.2 Every Super App MUST be accessible in **195 countries** (subject to geo-block exceptions in Article V).
1.3 UX MUST meet WCAG 2.2 AA accessibility standards.
1.4 Offline-first capability is REQUIRED for apps targeting markets with <30% reliable broadband.

## ARTICLE II — $SPI MONETARY MANDATE

2.1 **$SPI is the ONLY accepted payment token** in all Super Apps.
2.2 `noForeignToken()` modifier is MANDATORY on every `pay()`, `buy()`, `transfer()`, `send()`, and `withdraw()` function.
2.3 Pi Coin (`0xDeAdBeEf...`) is hard-rejected at the contract level — `require(token != PI_COIN, "PiCoin rejected")`.
2.4 All fee revenue, yield, and rewards MUST be denominated and paid in $SPI.
2.5 Fiat on/off ramps are permitted via GlobalFiatRegistry.sol — all conversions settle in $SPI.

## ARTICLE III — ETHICAL FINANCE

3.1 **Riba (interest) is PROHIBITED.** All lending is profit-share (Mudarabah) or cost-plus (Murabaha).
3.2 **Gharar (excessive uncertainty) is PROHIBITED.** All contracts must specify clear terms.
3.3 **Maysir (gambling) is PROHIBITED.** Prediction markets must be purely analytics-based, no wagering.
3.4 Yield products must be backed by real assets (sukuk, T-bills, real estate, commodities).
3.5 Halal certification is auto-generated by LEX_MACHINA Agent for compliant apps.

## ARTICLE IV — LEGAL & REGULATORY

4.1 **MiCA (EU)**: All apps operating in EU jurisdictions comply with MiCA Title III & IV.
4.2 **SEC/FinCEN (USA)**: All apps comply with the Bank Secrecy Act, AML/KYC requirements.
4.3 **FATF Travel Rule**: All transfers >$1,000 USD equivalent include originator/beneficiary info.
4.4 **GDPR/PDPA**: Personal data is processed with explicit consent; right to erasure is honored.
4.5 **Shariah Board Review**: Finance/DeFi/banking apps require Shariah board sign-off before launch.

## ARTICLE V — GEO-BLOCKING

5.1 Geo-blocked by default (OFAC/UN sanctions): North Korea, Iran, Syria, Cuba, Russia (financial services), Belarus, Myanmar.
5.2 Geo-blocks are configurable by NEXUS_ROLE via LEX_MACHINA's GeopoliticalFilter.sol.
5.3 No Super App may be used to circumvent international sanctions.

## ARTICLE VI — DATA SOVEREIGNTY

6.1 User data is stored in the user's jurisdiction by default (data localization).
6.2 On-chain data is immutable and transparent; off-chain data is encrypted with user-controlled keys.
6.3 No Super App may sell user data to third parties.
6.4 Biometric data (if used) requires explicit consent and is stored only as ZK-attested commitments.

## ARTICLE VII — DEVELOPER OBLIGATIONS

7.1 All Super App contracts must be formally verified before deployment.
7.2 All Super Apps must pass CI/CD gate: Pi Coin check + noForeignToken + riba check + i18n completeness.
7.3 All Super Apps must be open-source under MIT license.
7.4 Security audits required for any contract managing >$1M $SPI TVL.

## ARTICLE VIII — AI & AUTOMATION

8.1 AI-generated content must be clearly labelled.
8.2 AI credit scoring must be explainable and auditable.
8.3 Autonomous agents (ARCHON-1 through -8) operate under NEXUS Prime authority; actions logged immutably.
8.4 No AI agent may take irreversible financial action on behalf of a user without explicit consent.

## ARTICLE IX — ENVIRONMENTAL

9.1 Super Pi L2 uses Proof-of-Stake consensus. All Super Apps inherit this zero-mining-waste guarantee.
9.2 Apps in energy/resource categories must demonstrate net-positive environmental impact.
