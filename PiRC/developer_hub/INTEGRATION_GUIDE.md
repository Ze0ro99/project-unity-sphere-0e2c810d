# 🏛️ Sovereign OS: Developer Integration Hub

Welcome to the Sovereign Economic System. Follow these steps to integrate your **Pi App Studio** project.

### 1. Auto-Import & Tokenization
By calling the `sync_studio_inventory` endpoint, all your app's products are imported into the Sovereign Registry and tokenized as RWAs.

### 2. Pioneer Liquidity Reward
Upon successful integration, you are automatically granted **5000 tokens from each of the 7 layers** (35,000 total). 
* Use these to establish the Liquidity Pool for your app-native token.

### 3. Automated Sovereign Swap & Trust
* **Visa/Token Integration:** Customers can pay via traditional Visa or any of the 7 Sovereign tokens. 
* **Auto-Conversion:** All payments are instantly swapped into your app's token through our smart contracts.
* **AML Compliance:** Every transaction is logged with its **Source, Product Type, and Time** to ensure a safe, legal, and trusted environment.
