# ⚖️ Consumer Protection & Risk Disclosure Framework

## 1. Automated Risk Warnings
In compliance with global standards, all frontend templates integrated via PiRC mandate terminal and GUI-based risk warnings prior to interaction with liquidity or RWA protocols.

## 2. The PiRC-Compensation-Vault (Redress Mechanism)
To address gaps in consumer protection, the **Regulated Vault Standard** enforces a time-locked collateral allocation.
- **Smart Contract Invariant:** `min_collateral > risk_exposure * 1.5`
- If an oracle detects a dispute anomaly, funds enter a 14-day lock, awaiting decentralized tribunal (governance) or institutional arbitration.

## 3. Stabilization Metrics
Adhering to the value protection clauses, `PiRC-101` standardizes mathematical constraints to prevent hyper-inflationary or deflationary spirals within the internal utility token borders.
